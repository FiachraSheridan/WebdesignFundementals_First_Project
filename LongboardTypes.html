Different Longboard Types

Drop through vs Topmounted

The first step to understanding the different types of longboards is to understand the two different ways the trucks can be mounted.

  • Top Mounted
Top Mounted boards are boards that have there trucks mounted on the underside of the board. They are usually high up above the ground, letting them make sharper turns, at the cost of stability.
  • Drop Through
Drop Through boards are boards that have the baseplate of the trucks protruding through to the top of the board, making the board lower to the ground. the lower center of gravity makes it more stable, and the board being lower to the ground makes it easier to push, at the cost of easier left and right mobility.
